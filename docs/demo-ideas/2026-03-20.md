### Demo 2: The Compliance Audit Trail — AI You Can Show Your LP Committee

**Tagline:** Every AI decision Winston makes is logged, attributed, and exportable for your compliance team — before your regulators ask.

**Target persona:** CFO / Controller

**Problem it solves:** Enterprise procurement teams at institutional REPE firms are blocking AI tool adoption because they can't answer "what did the AI decide, why, and who was responsible?" The EU AI Act (Aug 2026) and Colorado AI Act (June 2026) require machine-readable audit trails for high-risk AI decisions. Firms without audit capabilities will fail compliance reviews — and lose deals to vendors who have them.

**Demo flow (7 steps):**
1. Open the Winston AI chat workspace and run a realistic query: *"What is the DSCR on [Asset X] and should we be concerned about the upcoming debt maturity?"*
2. Winston responds with a structured answer — DSCR pulled from the Debt tab, maturity date, risk flag
3. Navigate to the new Governance / Audit Log tab (top nav)
4. Show the per-request audit log entry: timestamp, user attribution, which MCP tools were invoked (e.g., `get_asset_debt_metrics`), what data was accessed, what the model returned, and what action the user took
5. Filter the log by "high-risk decisions" — surface flagged outputs where Winston recommended action
6. Click "Export Audit Report" → PDF downloads in 3 seconds, formatted for LP committee or external review
7. Show the Compliance Dashboard widget: total AI decisions this quarter, override rate, flagged outputs, data sources used

**Winston capabilities shown:**
- Full MCP tool invocation logging
- Per-request chain-of-custody: user → tool → data → model output → user action
- Searchable audit log with risk filtering
- PDF/CSV export for compliance reporting
- Compliance dashboard widget

**The "wow moment":** The CFO clicks "Export Audit Report" and a formatted, LP-ready PDF appears in 3 seconds — with every AI decision from the quarter traceable to a specific user, a specific data source, and a specific tool call.

**Data needed:** Audit log database (new), sample AI interactions to populate the log with realistic entries. Seed with 30–50 demo queries and their tool invocations.

**Build status:** Needs build — audit middleware, log database, export PDF, and dashboard widget. This is the top-priority feature per the Feature Radar (score: 9). Runway before EU AI Act enforcement: ~4 months.

**Sales angle:** This is the demo that unlocks institutional procurement — CFOs at firms with European LPs will require this before signing any AI contract. No REPE competitor currently offers it. Show this and JunieAI has no answer.

### Demo 3: UW vs Actual Triage — 33 Assets, Flagged in 30 Seconds

**Tagline:** Winston scans your entire portfolio, finds every asset diverging from underwriting, and tells you exactly why — before your Monday morning call.

**Target persona:** Asset Manager / CFO

**Problem it solves:** Asset managers at firms with 20–50 assets spend 4–6 hours per quarter manually pulling UW vs Actual comparisons, building variance tables in Excel, and writing explanations for the investment committee. With rates higher-for-longer and cap rates compressing, the cost of missing a variance early — a deteriorating DSCR, a trailing NOI — is higher than ever. One missed flag on a $50M asset can mean a missed refinancing window.

**Demo flow (9 steps):**
1. Open the Reports tab → UW vs Actual view
2. Show the fund comparison: IGF-VII with 33 active assets, performance vs underwriting
3. Open the Winston AI workspace and type: *"Scan all 33 assets. Flag any where NOI is more than 10% below underwriting. Rank by severity."*
4. Winston streams back a ranked list: Asset A (−18% NOI vs UW), Asset B (−14%), Asset C (−11%), with MCP tools pulling live financials in real time
5. Click Asset A — Winston has pre-generated a two-paragraph explanation: "NOI shortfall driven by occupancy decline from 94% to 87% and $280K in unbudgeted CapEx. DSCR now at 1.12x vs underwritten 1.35x. Debt maturity in 14 months."
6. Ask: *"What's the refinancing risk if rates stay at 3.75% for 12 more months?"* — Winston runs a scenario and returns a debt coverage stress test
7. Navigate to the Debt tab for the flagged asset — show the live DSCR, LTV, and amortization schedule matching the AI output
8. Winston offers: *"Draft a one-page asset update for the investment committee?"* — generates in 8 seconds
9. Show the audit trail for this analysis (feeds into Demo 2)

**Winston capabilities shown:**
- UW vs Actual report surface (live)
- Portfolio-wide MCP tool scan (33 assets simultaneously)
- Asset-level financials: NOI, DSCR, LTV, debt maturity
- Scenario modeling (rate sensitivity)
- Investment committee memo generation

**The "wow moment":** Asking Winston to scan 33 assets and rank variances — and watching it return a prioritized list with explanations in under 20 seconds. An asset manager's entire Monday morning prep, compressed to half a minute.

**Data needed:** Live UW vs Actual data for all 33 assets (currently seeded). Needs realistic variance across ~5 assets to make the scan compelling. Flag: ensure at least 3 assets show >10% NOI shortfall in demo data.

**Build status:** Mostly ready — UW vs Actual report and asset financials are live. Portfolio-wide MCP scan requires a `compare_uw_vs_actual_portfolio` tool (likely not built — verify). Committee memo generation works via chat today.

**Sales angle:** This demo closes asset managers — they see their exact Monday morning problem solved, live, with their own fund's structure reflected back at them. The 30-second wow moment maps directly to a real pain they feel weekly.

A $100M deal with a floating-rate assumption needs a different answer today than it did in December.

**Demo flow (7 steps):**
1. Open the Deal Radar tab — show the radar chart with 8 active deals totaling $474M across risk/return quadrants

6. The radar chart updates to reflect the re-ranked deals (or Winston describes the repositioning clearly)
7. Ask: *"Draft a 3-bullet deal memo update for the IC summarizing our rate risk exposure across the pipeline."*

**Winston capabilities shown:**
- Deal Radar visualization with 8 live deals

- Real-time scenario modeling against macro inputs
- Portfolio-level risk ranking
- IC memo generation

**The "wow moment":** Winston takes a real news event (the March 18 Fed decision) and applies it to the firm's actual deal pipeline in under 20 seconds — producing a ranked risk list that a GP would normally need an analyst to build over a morning.

**Data needed:** 8 deals with cap rate assumptions, financing terms, and IRR models in the Deal Radar (currently seeded at $474M). Ensure at least 2–3 deals have floating-rate exposure for the rate sensitivity scenario to land.

### Demo 5: The Accuracy Scorecard — Proof That Winston Is Right

**Tagline:** Every answer Winston gives shows exactly where it came from — and you can show your LP committee a quarterly proof of accuracy.

**Target persona:** CFO / GP (institutional procurement audience)

**Problem it solves:** The #1 fear about AI (per Anthropic's 81K-user survey) is "getting things wrong." Institutional REPE firms — especially those with pension fund or endowment LPs — face LP scrutiny over AI governance. The objection isn't "we don't want AI," it's "how do we know it's right?" No REPE competitor currently answers this question with data.

**Demo flow (6 steps):**
1. Run any Winston query: *"What is the current cap rate on [Asset X] and how does it compare to our acquisition underwriting?"*
2. Show the response — then point to the new "Grounding Score" badge below the answer: "97% — sourced from firm data"
3. Expand the grounding detail: shows exactly which data sources were used (Asset Financials, Valuation module, Original UW model), which MCP tools were invoked, and confidence level
4. Ask a question that pushes into general knowledge: *"What's the typical cap rate for class-B multifamily in Atlanta right now?"* — grounding score drops to 45% (sourced from general knowledge, not firm data), with a clear warning flag
5. Navigate to the Accuracy Dashboard: "This quarter, Winston answered 2,847 REPE questions. 94.2% were grounded in verified firm data. Override rate: 3.1%."
6. Click "Generate LP Accuracy Report" — formatted PDF that GPs can attach to their LP annual report or share with compliance teams

**Winston capabilities shown:**
- Per-response grounding score with data source attribution
- Visual confidence indicator on every AI output
- Accuracy Dashboard (aggregate metrics)
- LP-ready PDF accuracy report
- Clear signaling when Winston is using firm data vs. general knowledge

**The "wow moment":** The CFO sees a "94.2% grounding in verified firm data" number on a dashboard — and can export it as a PDF to show their LP committee. This turns "can we trust AI?" from a philosophical question into a quarterly metric.

**Data needed:** Grounding score metadata on each AI response (requires AI gateway enhancement). Seed the Accuracy Dashboard with realistic sample data: 2,847 queries, 94.2% grounding rate. The PDF export generates from live dashboard data.

**Build status:** Needs build — requires AI gateway enhancement (confidence scoring), new response metadata, Accuracy Dashboard widget, and PDF export. Complexity is Medium per Feature Radar. Can ship incrementally — start with quantitative outputs (cap rates, IRRs, DSCR) where accuracy is provable.

**Sales angle:** This demo wins institutional buyers whose procurement teams require AI governance proof. It directly addresses the single biggest sales objection — before the prospect raises it.
