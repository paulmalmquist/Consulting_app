## Recommendations

**Priority 1 — Fix AI Chat 500 error:** The conversation creation endpoint is returning a server error. Check Railway backend logs for the `/api/v1/ai/conversations` (or equivalent) endpoint. This is a regression from 3/24 and blocks the most important demo capability.

**Priority 2 — Fix equity fund performance calculations:** The TVPI/IRR calculation is clearly ignoring unrealized portfolio value or using wrong inputs. The AI summary card appears to have a separate, possibly correct calculation. Align the two — either the card is right and the KPIs need fixing, or both need investigation.

**Priority 3 — Fix Paid-In > Committed anomaly:** Capital call totals ($935M across 4 calls) don't justify $1.3B+ paid-in values on the equity funds. The paid-in field may be double-counting or pulling from a wrong source.

**Priority 4 — Seed investment sub-records:** Property_type, market, valuation, and operating_data remain absent. This was P4 on 3/24 and hasn't been addressed.

**Priority 5 — Seed distribution payout rows:** The Total Paid KPI fix is great, but individual event detail still shows 0 payout rows. Seed actual payout rows so the Largest Recipients panel populates.
