# Offer C: AI Fluency Workshop

## Positioning
**Title:** AI Fluency for Business Leaders
**Value Prop:** "Your team will leave with a practical AI playbook specific to your industry, not generic ChatGPT tips."
**Category:** Workshop / training (conversion vehicle)

## Target Buyer
- Executive teams, department heads, business owners
- Aware AI matters but unsure how to evaluate or adopt it
- Often prompted by board questions, competitor moves, or team requests
- Any industry, but strongest fit for operations-heavy businesses

## Scope
Half-day or full-day workshop including:
1. AI landscape overview (what's real, what's hype, what's coming)
2. Live demonstrations using real business workflows
3. Hands-on exercises with AI tools
4. Industry-specific use case workshop
5. AI opportunity assessment for their specific business
6. 30-day action plan creation

## Deliverables
- Workshop materials and reference guide
- AI opportunity assessment customized to their business
- 30-day action plan with specific next steps
- Post-workshop follow-up call (2 weeks after)
- Optional: Diagnostic offer presentation at end of workshop

## Timeline
- Pre-work: 30-minute intake call to customize content
- Workshop: Half-day (4 hours) or full-day (7 hours)
- Follow-up: 2-week check-in call

## Pricing
- **Half-Day Workshop:** $3,500
- **Full-Day Workshop:** $6,000
- **Full-Day + Post-Workshop Diagnostic:** $10,000
- **Multi-company open workshop (per seat):** $500-$750

## Conversion Strategy
The workshop is designed as a conversion vehicle:
1. Workshop reveals specific AI opportunities in their business
2. Post-workshop assessment quantifies the opportunity
3. Follow-up call presents diagnostic offer (Offer A) as natural next step
4. Target: 30-50% workshop → diagnostic conversion rate
