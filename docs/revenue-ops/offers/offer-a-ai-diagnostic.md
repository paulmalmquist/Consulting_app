# Offer A: AI Operations Diagnostic

## Positioning
**Title:** AI Operations Diagnostic
**Value Prop:** "In two weeks, we'll map exactly where AI saves your team the most time and money, and implement your first quick win before we leave."
**Category:** Consulting engagement (entry-level)

## Target Buyer
- COO, VP of Operations, or business owner at mid-market firms (50-500 employees)
- Currently managing workflows manually that could be automated
- Has heard about AI but doesn't know where to start or who to trust
- South Florida bias for in-person relationship advantage

## Scope
1. Process audit: Map 5-10 core workflows with time/cost analysis
2. AI opportunity scoring: Rate each workflow for automation feasibility and ROI
3. Prioritized roadmap: Rank opportunities by impact vs. effort
4. Quick win implementation: Build and deploy one automation during the engagement
5. Executive presentation: Findings, recommendations, and roadmap for leadership

## Deliverables
- Workflow audit report (5-10 workflows documented)
- AI opportunity matrix (feasibility × impact scoring)
- Prioritized implementation roadmap (3-6 month horizon)
- One working automation (delivered and tested)
- Executive presentation deck
- 30-day follow-up check-in call

## Timeline
- Week 1: Intake interviews, workflow mapping, data collection
- Week 2: Analysis, quick win build, report creation, executive presentation

## Pricing
- **Diagnostic Only:** $7,500
- **Diagnostic + First Implementation:** $15,000
- **Diagnostic + 90-Day Implementation Program:** $35,000-$50,000 (scoped after diagnostic)

## Required Inputs from Client
- Access to 2-3 team members for 60-minute workflow interviews
- List of top 10 recurring manual workflows or reports
- Sample outputs/reports from those workflows
- Brief on current tools and systems in use

## Proof Points Required
- 2 workflow transformation examples (before/after with time savings)
- ROI calculator showing typical 10-30x return on diagnostic fee
- Diagnostic questionnaire (used during intake)

## Success Criteria
- Client has a clear, prioritized AI roadmap they can act on
- At least one working automation saving 5+ hours/week
- Client agrees to next phase (implementation program) within 30 days of completion

## Objection Handling
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We can just use ChatGPT" | "ChatGPT is a tool. We're offering a strategy and implementation. The diagnostic shows you WHERE to apply AI, not just that AI exists." |
| "Too expensive for just an assessment" | "You're not paying for an assessment. You're paying for a working automation and a roadmap. The quick win alone typically saves $7,500+ in the first quarter." |
| "We're not ready for AI" | "That's exactly why the diagnostic exists. It tells you what you're ready for now, what needs prep work, and what to sequence for later." |
| "Can you just tell us what to do?" | "We can. But without seeing your actual workflows, any advice is generic. The diagnostic is customized to your operations." |
