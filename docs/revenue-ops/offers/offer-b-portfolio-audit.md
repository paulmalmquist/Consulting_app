# Offer B: Portfolio Intelligence Audit

## Positioning
**Title:** Portfolio Intelligence Audit
**Value Prop:** "We'll show you exactly where your portfolio data is fragmented and build the unified view your LPs have been asking for."
**Category:** Consulting engagement (REPE-specific)

## Target Buyer
- CFO, Head of Portfolio Operations, or CTO at REPE firms ($500M-$5B AUM)
- Managing 3+ data sources (Yardi, Excel, Juniper Square, internal tools)
- Under LP pressure for better reporting transparency
- In fundraising mode or approaching fund close

## Scope
1. Data source inventory: Map every system, spreadsheet, and manual process in the reporting chain
2. Reporting gap analysis: Identify missing metrics, delayed reports, and manual reconciliation steps
3. LP transparency scorecard: Benchmark current reporting against institutional LP expectations
4. Unified dashboard prototype: Build a working prototype showing consolidated portfolio view
5. Implementation roadmap: 90-day plan for achieving full data unification

## Deliverables
- Data architecture assessment document
- LP reporting gap matrix (current vs. institutional standard)
- Working prototype dashboard (built in Winston)
- 90-day implementation roadmap with milestones
- Executive presentation with findings and recommendations

## Timeline
- Week 1-2: Data source audit, stakeholder interviews, gap analysis
- Week 3: Prototype build, LP scorecard
- Week 4: Final report, executive presentation, roadmap delivery

## Pricing
- **Audit Only:** $25,000
- **Audit + Prototype Implementation:** $75,000
- **Audit + Full Implementation (90-day):** $100,000-$150,000 (scoped after audit)

## Required Inputs from Client
- Access to current reporting systems and sample outputs
- Sample LP reports (quarterly, annual)
- Fund structure overview (number of funds, vehicles, asset types)
- 2-3 stakeholder interviews (CFO, asset management, investor relations)

## Success Criteria
- Client sees data gaps quantified with business impact
- Working prototype demonstrates unified view is achievable
- Client commits to implementation phase within 30 days
