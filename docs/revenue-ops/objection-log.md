## Objection Tracking Rules

1. Every objection heard in a live conversation gets logged with date, source, and exact wording
2. Anticipated objections (from research/competitor intel) are logged separately and tagged as such
3. Counters are tested in conversations and updated based on what actually works
4. Objections heard 3+ times get promoted to the offer sheet and outreach templates
5. Friday review adds new objections; Tuesday proof asset work refines counters
