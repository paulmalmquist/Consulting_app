### 1. "Winston Playbooks" — Counter to 3 Competitors at Once
Both Yardi (Virtuoso Marketplace), Cherre (Agent.STUDIO), and Procore (Agent Builder) are shipping user-facing agent/automation builders. Winston has the underlying infrastructure (31 MCP tool categories, AI Gateway, agentic build surface) but no user-facing "build your own workflow" UX. A "Winston Playbooks" surface would counter all three with one feature.

**Sales talking point:** "While they're selling agent builders, we ship pre-built intelligence workflows that work on day one — no prompt engineering required."

Dealpath Connect is a network-effect marketplace that Winston can't replicate with code. The positioning play is: "Dealpath tells you *what deals exist*. Winston tells you *which deals to do* and *how they'll perform*." Consider a Dealpath integration story.
