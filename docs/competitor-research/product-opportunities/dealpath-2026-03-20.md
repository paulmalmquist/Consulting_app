## Top Gaps (Not in Winston, High Enterprise Value)

1. **IC Memo Generation from Deal Data** (Easy build, 3-5 days) — Investment committee prep is a major time sink. Auto-generating IC teasers and memos from structured deal data in Winston would save analysts hours per deal. Very high demo impact.

2. **AI Data Extract for OMs** (Partial, 1 week) — OM abstraction is the most requested AI feature in CRE deal management. Dealpath claims 95% accuracy on 90+ fields in under 1 minute. Winston's document pipeline needs OM-specific extraction templates.

3. **AI Recommended Comps** (Moderate, 2 weeks) — Automated comp identification accelerates underwriting significantly. Winston needs a transaction database or market data feed to power this.

4. **CRM with AI Summaries** (Moderate, 2-3 weeks) — Relationship intelligence is increasingly valued. Purpose-built real estate investor CRM with AI context summaries would be a strong differentiator.

## Quick Wins (Already Partial — Just Need Assembly)

1. **IC Memo Generation** — Winston has deal data and AI. Build a "Generate IC Memo" MCP tool that pulls structured fields (property, price, cap rate, NOI, thesis) into a templated memo document.

2. **AI Data Extract for OMs** — Extend document ingestion pipeline with OM-specific field schema (90+ fields including property name, address, SF, units, asking price, cap rate, NOI, tenant list).

3. **Underwriting Model Comparison** — Winston has scenario modeling. Add version tracking and side-by-side comparison UI for underwriting assumptions.

4. **Deal Execution Milestones** — Add LOI, PSA, IC approval, and closing milestone tracking to Deal Radar with smart reminders via SSE notifications.
