## Top Gaps (Not in Winston, High Enterprise Value)

1. **Covenant Breach Alerting** — CFOs and asset managers need automated covenant breach alerts. Winston has all the DSCR/LTV data to drive this. High-value, low-effort build.
2. **PM System Data Connector** — The ability to pull live from Voyager/MRI/AppFolio is Yardi's biggest structural advantage. Without this, firms with Voyager won't replace Yardi. This is a strategic moat question.
3. **Deal Stage Workflow + Team Collaboration** — Acquisition teams need structured workflows, not just a visualization. Winston's Deal Radar is ahead on UX but behind on workflow depth.
4. **LP Investor Portal** — Same gap as vs. Juniper Square. LP portal is table-stakes for a fund management platform.

## Quick Wins (Already Partial — Just Need Assembly)

1. **Covenant Breach Alert Engine** — DSCR/LTV data is in Winston. A rules engine that compares values against covenant thresholds and fires an alert (email + in-app notification) is 3-5 days.
2. **Capital Call + Distribution Notice Generation** — Same quick win as vs. JS; the calc layer exists.
3. **Role-Based Dashboard Views** — Winston dashboards exist. Adding a "GP view" vs "Asset Manager view" with filtered metric sets is a settings/access control build, not a data build.
4. **Benchmark Reporting** — Winston's UW vs Actual is already a comparison report. Extending it to compare against market benchmarks (NCREIF, fund targets) requires a reference data source and 1 week of build.
