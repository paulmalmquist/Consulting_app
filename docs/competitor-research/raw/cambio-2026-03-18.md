## Product Modules / Capabilities

- **Retrofit Identification & Execution** — Surface ROI-ranked retrofit opportunities across portfolios, coordinate implementation across asset managers, property managers, building engineers, consultancies
- **Compliance & Reporting** — Automated reporting to GRESB, TCFD, SFDR, EnergyStar, regulatory benchmarking, and investor reporting
- **Data Ingestion** — Extract building data from PDFs and spreadsheets; AI-powered parsing; connect to thousands of utility providers
- **Integration Layer** — EnergyStar Portfolio Manager, corporate data lake via API
- **Escalation & Collaboration Workflows** — Automated stakeholder data collection, decision approval workflows, shared outcome tracking
- **Proactive Tenant Outreach** — Automated outreach to tenants for data collection
