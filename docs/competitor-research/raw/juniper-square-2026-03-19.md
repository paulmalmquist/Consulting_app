## Product Modules / Capabilities

- **AI CRM for Investor Relations** — purpose-built CRM for private markets GP/IR teams
- **Investor Onboarding** — digital subscription workflows, automated document extraction and validation
- **Portal** — shared LP/GP environment; self-service investor access to metrics and documents
- **Investor Reporting** — industry-standard report templates for any fund size/type
- **Insights** — converts partnership data into actionable insights dashboards
- **Data Rooms** — secure document sharing for fundraising/deal contexts
- **Distribution Payments** — payment initiation, management, and tracking
- **API Integrations** — event-driven API for custom integrations
- **Waterfall Calculations** — automated waterfall allocations and investor equity calculations (NAV, NIA, investor shares)
- **AML/KYC compliance** — managed compliance workflow
- **Nasdaq eVestment Integration** (launching summer 2026) — access to 100,000+ investor/consultant contacts across 30,000+ profiles directly in the CRM; no separate API dev required
- **Fund Administration Services** — fund accounting, treasury services, investor services, managed close
- **Capital Activity Automation** — capital calls, distributions, investor position transfers
- **Two-way Email/Calendar Sync** — Google and Microsoft integrations that auto-update CRM contact records, eliminating manual data entry

## AI / Automation Features

- **JunieAI** — "the first enterprise-grade AI purpose-built for private market GPs"
  - Chat with structured and unstructured data ("ask questions, get answers, get work done")
  - Converts investor communications (emails, memos, meetings) into actionable next steps
  - Document processing: extract, validate, and load data from subscription documents, invoices, K-1 forms
  - LP onboarding automation: 70% reduction in manual data entry (claimed)
  - Cash reconciliation automation
  - DDQ (Due Diligence Questionnaire) response drafting
  - LP email drafting
  - Quarterly report compilation
  - Meeting note summarization
  - Key term extraction from legal documents
  - Activity monitoring: surfaces LP relationship signals for IR teams
  - Governance layer: visibility and control over AI agent activities
  - Accuracy claim: "compliance-grade outputs" with hallucination elimination
  - Positioned as agent infrastructure, not isolated chat window
