## Product Modules / Capabilities

**Yardi Investment Suite — Four Core Products:**
1. **Investment Manager** — branded investor portal, automated capital raising and LP onboarding, capital transactions and investor activity tracking, CRM
2. **Investment Accounting** — automated real estate ownership and alternative asset accounting, financial consolidation and eliminations
3. **Debt Manager** — real estate loan management, centralized loan data, covenant and collateral tracking, simplified covenant compliance
4. **Acquisition Manager** — deal pipeline management, centralized deal tracking, customizable acquisition/disposition workflows

**Cross-Suite Capabilities:**
- Complex ownership structure support (fund-of-funds, JVs, tiered structures)
- Investor portal with self-service access to metrics and documents
- Configurable dashboards (role-based access)
- Time-weighted returns and IRR calculations
- Capital call automation
- Distribution payment processing
- Loan transaction posting
- Deal room for new investment opportunities (subscription/onboarding)
- Performance Manager module (analytics layer)

**Yardi Virtuoso** — AI platform that "animates" the entire Yardi product suite; positioned as an infrastructure layer unlocking AI across all products, not a standalone product
