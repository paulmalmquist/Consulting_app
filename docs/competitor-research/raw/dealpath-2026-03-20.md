## Product Modules / Capabilities

- **Pipeline Management** — Real-time deal flow tracking from sourcing through closing; structured pipeline capturing with key assumption mapping
- **Deal Execution** — Centralized data, automated workflows, full visibility from LOI to close; task management, document management, deadline tracking
- **AI Studio** — Purpose-built AI capabilities for CRE investment workflows
- **CRM** — Purpose-built for real estate investors; track broker, lender, and capital partner relationships; AI summaries of relationship and activity history
- **Reporting & Analytics** — Real-time dashboards; slice and dice pipeline, portfolio, and execution metrics
- **Market Tracking** — Proprietary comparables database benchmarking deal financials; MSCI/Real Capital Analytics integration
- **Dealpath Connect** — Private exchange for real estate listings; digitized deal distribution connecting investment firms with top brokerages; 65% of on-/off-market institutional listings; ~19,000 deals, $930B aggregate value; integrated with CBRE, JLL, and other leading brokers
- **Underwriting Tools** — Underwriting model comparison; versioned underwriting models; structured, reportable assumptions
- **Document Management** — IC memo generation, LOI creation, deal collateral storage; audit-ready documentation
- **Approval Workflows** — Built-in approval steps; structured approval mechanisms; investment committee preparation tools

- **AI Deal Screening** — Instant market, tenant, and property insights on Dealpath Connect listings; reduces screening time from hours to minutes
- **AI Recommended Comps** — Automatically identifies optimal comparables based on price, proximity, square footage using proprietary and third-party market data; accelerates underwriting
- **AI Data Extract** — Automates OM and flyer abstraction; under 1 minute extraction time; 95% accuracy for 90+ listing & property fields; launched 2024, enhanced continuously
- **AI Summaries in CRM** — Distills relationship and activity history for companies, contacts, and deals
- Smart reminders and automated task handoffs
