- **ARGUS Enterprise** — Leading CRE valuation and cash flow forecasting software (now integrated into ARGUS Intelligence as core component)
- **ARGUS EstateMaster** — Property development feasibility and management software
- **ARGUS Developer** — Real estate development software for complex, multi-stage projects
- **ARGUS Taliance** — Real estate fund management software: model and manage performance of real estate funds
- **ARGUS ValueInsight** — Valuation management platform covering entire asset valuation lifecycle
- **Altus Data Studio** — Data analytics platform
