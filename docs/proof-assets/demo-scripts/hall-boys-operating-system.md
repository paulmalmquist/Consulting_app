# Hall Boys Operating System

## Demo Script

1. Open `Hall Boys Operating System` from the authenticated environment list.
   Say: "This is your entire business across five operating companies in one execution surface."

2. Start on Executive.
   Point to March 2026 revenue at `$12.5M`, weighted margin at `12.0%`, cash at `$4.1M`, and `2` at-risk projects.

3. Explain what is going wrong.
   Call out Logistics margin compression, Airport Expansion overrun, and New Development Site A blocking revenue recognition.

4. Drill into `Airport Expansion`.
   Show budget vs actual, the electrical/materials overrun, linked documents, and the close task waiting on Apex approval.

5. Open Vendors.
   Show Apex Electrical and Prime Staffing duplicated across entities with spend above contract coverage.

6. Open Documents.
   Use the Apex Electrical contract and point out the `$250K` payment due within `30 days`, the `auto-renewal` clause, and the penalty language.

7. Ask Winston: `What should I focus on today?`
   Expected grounded answer: unblock Logistics invoices, resolve Site A revenue recognition, and contain the two red projects.

8. Close with the punchline.
   Say: "This replaces fragmented reporting, coordination, and analysis with one operating system."
