## paulmalmquist.com — Winston App Landing Page

### Current State

The page is a minimal login gateway with:
- Tagline: *"AI execution environment for real estate private equity, project delivery, and institutional operations."*
- Two login buttons (Admin / Environment)
- "Request a walkthrough" CTA linking to novendor.ai/contact
- Meta: `noindex, nofollow` — intentionally not a public marketing page

### Key Gaps: Massive Underselling

The landing page mentions **4 capabilities** in metadata. The platform has **258 pages, 208 backend services, 31 MCP tool categories, and 32 lab environment types.** The gap is extreme.

**1. Replace the tagline with a capability-dense version:**
- Current: *"AI execution environment for real estate private equity, project delivery, and institutional operations."*
- Proposed: *"AI execution environment — fund reporting, waterfall engines, deal scoring, credit decisioning, capital projects, document extraction, LP analytics, and 32 configurable lab environments."*

**2. Add a "Platform at a Glance" stat bar below the hero:**
```
258 pages · 208 services · 31 AI tool categories · 32 lab environments
```
This immediately signals enterprise-grade depth to anyone logging in or evaluating.

**3. Add a capabilities ribbon (one line of scrolling tags):**
`Fund Management · Deal Radar · LP Waterfall · Credit Decisioning · Capital Projects · PDS Analytics · Document Extraction · Monte Carlo Simulation · ESG Modules · CRM · Compliance & Audit`

**4. Add social proof line above "Request a walkthrough":**
- *"Purpose-built for institutional operators managing $100M+ portfolios"* (or similar credibility anchor)

**5. OG/meta description upgrade:**
- Current: *"Fund reporting, waterfall logic, capital activity, and portfolio monitoring."*
- Proposed: *"AI execution environment for REPE, credit, and institutional ops. Fund waterfall engines, deal scoring, LP analytics, credit decisioning, capital project draws, document extraction, Monte Carlo simulation, and 32 configurable lab environments."*
