For equities, the breakdown below the two hundred day moving average shifts the default posture from buy-dips to reduce-exposure. Cyclicals, small caps, and high-multiple growth names face the most headwind. Defensive sectors — utilities, consumer staples, healthcare — are better positioned.

For commodities, the strengthening dollar creates headwinds for dollar-denominated commodities. Gold may benefit from safe-haven flows but is not immune to risk-off selling in acute stress scenarios.

For crypto, the BTC-SPX correlation rebound means Bitcoin is unlikely to decouple positively from equity weakness in the near term. Risk-off in equities is likely to pressure crypto unless macro conditions shift materially.

The liquidity floor from M2 expansion means this regime is unlikely to morph into RISK_OFF_PANIC without a new catalyst. Watch VIX above thirty-five, HY spreads above four hundred basis points, or a dollar spike above one hundred and five as escalation signals.
