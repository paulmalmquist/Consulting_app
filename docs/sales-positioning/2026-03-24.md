## Quick-Draw Battlecard

### vs Juniper Square
**Lead with:** "Juniper Square is announcing a new data integration every quarter — eVestment this summer, Preqin before that. Each one adds a screen. Winston adds a capability: any data source becomes actionable across your entire investment lifecycle through our AI gateway."
**Acknowledge:** "JS has strong private markets fundraising DNA and now covers credit strategies via Tenor. If you're running a private credit fund, they deserve a hard look."
**Kill shot:** "Ask them what happens to your fund management, asset reporting, and deal pipeline when you use JS. The answer is 'use another tool.' With Winston, it's all one platform."

### vs Yardi
**Lead with:** "Yardi's new Virtuoso AI helps a property manager schedule maintenance. Winston's AI helps an investment manager model a waterfall, draft an LP letter, and flag a covenant breach. Different AI, different buyer, different value."
**Acknowledge:** "Yardi's breadth across property management and accounting is unmatched for operators. If you're managing properties first and investments second, Yardi makes sense."
**Kill shot:** "Yardi is spending 2026 migrating to modern. Winston was born modern. Ask them when their Voyager 8 migration completes."

### vs Cherre
**Lead with:** "Cherre just launched an agent builder for real estate AI. Impressive — but you still have to build and configure everything. Winston ships with the business logic already built: fund management, deal pipeline, investor reporting, portfolio analytics, all AI-wired."
**Acknowledge:** "Cherre's knowledge graph and data unification are genuinely powerful. Agent.STUDIO is innovative. If you're a large enterprise that wants to build custom AI workflows on your own data, they're a real option."
**Kill shot:** "After Cherre, you still need a platform to run your business. That's us. We're not the data layer — we're the operating system."

### vs Altus/ARGUS
**Lead with:** "ARGUS just sunset their standalone product — all clients are being migrated to ARGUS Intelligence. That migration is your window. Winston is the AI-native alternative: you get everything ARGUS Intelligence offers on valuation, plus fund management, deal pipeline, LP reporting, and 31 MCP tool categories. One upgrade instead of two."
**Acknowledge:** "ARGUS valuation methodology is industry-standard and trusted by appraisers and lenders. We're not replacing ARGUS — we position as the platform that makes ARGUS outputs actionable."
**Kill shot:** "ARGUS gives you a valuation. Winston gives you the decision — with the fund context, the LP implications, and the next action already drafted."

### vs Dealpath
**Lead with:** "Dealpath is the best deal tracker in the market — 19,000 deals, $930B on platform. We don't compete on deal sourcing. We compete on what happens to those deals for the next 10 years. Dealpath is where deals come in. Winston is where they live."
**Acknowledge:** "Dealpath Connect's brokerage marketplace and MSCI RCA integration are real moats. If sourcing and screening is 80% of your workflow, Dealpath wins that conversation."
**Kill shot:** "Ask them what Dealpath does after you close. The answer is 'export to another system.' With Winston, closing a deal is the beginning, not the end."

## New Objection Handlers (Updated 2026-03-24)

**"Juniper Square announced eVestment investor search — that's 100,000 LP profiles"**
"That's a great sourcing tool for fundraising teams. Winston isn't the place you find new LPs — it's the platform you run your fund on after you've closed them. Those are different jobs."

**"Cherre now lets us build custom AI agents"**
"Agent.STUDIO is impressive tech. But you're now buying a developer platform, not a business application. Winston ships with the business logic already built. The question is: do you want to spend quarters configuring agents, or do you want to run your portfolio Monday morning?"

**"We're being migrated from ARGUS Enterprise to ARGUS Intelligence"**
"That migration is actually your chance to reconsider. ARGUS Intelligence is a valuation platform expanding into investment management. Winston is an investment management platform that includes valuation modeling. Before you auto-upgrade, ask whether you want a valuation tool that's trying to become a platform, or a platform that was born for the job."

**"Yardi just shipped Virtuoso AI"**
"Virtuoso automates property operations — maintenance, invoicing, tenant communication. That's useful if you're operating properties. Winston's AI is for investment management: modeling, fund reporting, deal scoring, LP communications. Ask your Yardi rep to show you the investment-management AI demo. They'll show you property ops."
