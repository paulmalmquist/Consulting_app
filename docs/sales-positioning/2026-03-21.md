## vs Juniper Square

**Their pitch:** "AI-powered fundraising and investor management with Preqin and eVestment integrations."

**Winston counter:** Juniper Square is fundraising-only. Winston covers the entire investment lifecycle — deal sourcing, underwriting, fund management, LP reporting, AND AI-powered analytics across all of it. Their new AI features (natural language queries, document extraction) are things Winston has had in production. Their Preqin integration is nice for fundraising teams, but Winston's AI can synthesize fund performance, run Monte Carlo simulations, and generate investor reports — all in one conversation.

**Killer line:** "Juniper Square helps you raise the fund. Winston helps you raise it, deploy it, manage it, and report on it."
