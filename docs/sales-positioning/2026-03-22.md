## Quick-Draw Battlecard

**Lead with:** "JS just launched AI CRM — we've had a full 9-service AI-native CRM for months. Their AI is bolted on; ours is foundational."
**Acknowledge:** "JS has strong data partnerships (Preqin, eVestment) that enrich fundraising workflows. We're building toward similar integrations."
**Kill shot:** "Ask them how many AI tool categories their platform supports. We have 31."

### vs Yardi
**Lead with:** "Yardi is spending 2026 modernizing a 40-year-old codebase with Voyager 8 and Virtuoso AI beta. Winston was born cloud-native with production AI."
**Acknowledge:** "Yardi's breadth across property management and accounting is unmatched. We're focused on investment intelligence, not property ops."
**Kill shot:** "Their AI is beta. Ours is production with 31 tool categories, structured response rendering, and RAG."

### vs Cherre
**Lead with:** "Cherre connects your data. Winston runs your business — AI, workflows, and operating surface in one platform."
**Acknowledge:** "Cherre's Agent.STUDIO is innovative — letting clients build custom AI agents. We're watching this space closely."
**Kill shot:** "After Cherre unifies your data, you still need a system to manage funds, assets, and investors. That's us."

### vs Altus/ARGUS
**Lead with:** "ARGUS just shipped portfolio scenario simulation in March 2026. Winston has had Monte Carlo, stress testing, and scenario engines integrated with the full investment lifecycle."
**Acknowledge:** "ARGUS valuation methodology is industry-standard. We're complementary, not competitive — Winston makes ARGUS outputs actionable."
**Kill shot:** "ARGUS gives you a valuation. Winston gives you the decision."

### vs Dealpath
**Lead with:** "Dealpath tracks deals. Winston manages the entire lifecycle — sourcing through fund reporting, all AI-powered."
**Acknowledge:** "Dealpath Connect's marketplace with CBRE and JLL creates network effects we can't easily match. Their MSCI RCA integration is solid."
**Kill shot:** "Ask what happens after the deal closes. With Dealpath, you export to another system. With Winston, you're already there."

## Objection Handling

**"But [competitor] has more clients"**
"They have more legacy installations. We have a modern architecture that deploys in days, not months. Ask their clients how long their last upgrade took."

**"We already use ARGUS/Yardi"**
"Great — keep using it. Winston sits on top and makes that data intelligent. We're not asking you to rip and replace; we're asking you to upgrade what you can do with what you already have."

**"Cherre gives us data from everywhere"**
"Data is table stakes. Intelligence is the differentiator. Winston doesn't just show you data — it reasons about your portfolio, flags risks, and drafts investor communications."

**"Dealpath has a mobile app now"**
"Mobile access matters. We're actively developing our mobile experience. But the real question is: do you want a mobile deal tracker or a mobile investment platform?"
