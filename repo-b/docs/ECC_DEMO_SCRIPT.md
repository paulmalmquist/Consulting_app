# Executive Command Center Demo

## How To Demo

1. Open `/lab/environments`.
2. Tap `Open Meridian Apex`.
3. The app lands in `/lab/env/{envId}/ecc` with the seeded Meridian Apex Holdings queue.
4. Use `Queue`, `Brief`, `Search`, and `Settings` from the bottom nav for the full demo loop.
5. If you need a clean reset, open `Settings` and tap `Reset Demo`.

## 2-Minute iPhone Script

1. Open Winston and tap `Open Meridian Apex`.
2. Land on `Queue` and point to `Red Alerts`: payroll funding risk, two VIP SLA misses, and the overdue software renewal.
3. Scroll to `Approvals` and open the `$72,000` change order.
4. Tap `Approve`, add or keep the note, and tap `Confirm approval`.
5. Tap `Delegate`, confirm the handoff to Daniel Ortiz, and mention that the delegation stays visible until closed.
6. Go back to `Queue`, open the board member VIP message about capital call timing, and tap `Reply / Mark Done`.
7. Return to `Queue` and show that the VIP SLA alert is gone from `Red Alerts`.
8. Open `Brief` and call out `Cash Today`, `Due 72h`, `Overdue`, and `Exposure`.
9. Return to the change order approval page and point to the `Finance Linker` match cards.
10. Finish in `Settings`, hit `Reset Demo`, then note the queue is back to the exact seeded state.
