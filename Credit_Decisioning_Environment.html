PERMITTED:
Assertions traced to specific passages in approved corpus documents. Each assertion requires a citation chain with document ID, passage reference, and exact excerpt. Chain status must be COMPLETE.
DENIED BY DEFAULT:
Internet sources, training knowledge, interpolation between documents, best guesses, probabilistic confidence without deterministic validation, any assertion where the citation chain is PARTIAL or BROKEN.
ENFORCEMENT:
